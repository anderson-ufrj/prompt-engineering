## Version Numbering Convention

- **MAJOR** (X.0.0): Fundamental life/career change
  - Examples: Student → Employed, Single → Married, Brazil → International
- **MINOR** (0.X.0): Significant milestone or skill acquisition
  - Examples: New major project, MSc enrollment, speaking engagement
- **PATCH** (0.0.X): Regular updates, tweaks, minor corrections
  - Examples: Career status update, goal refinement, tone adjustment

---

## Update Triggers

### Immediate Updates (PATCH)
- Career status change (application, interview, offer)
- Goal completion or new short-term goal
- Discovery of communication pattern (good/bad example)

### Regular Updates (MINOR)
- Completion of major project milestone
- Acquisition of significant new skill
- Change in learning focus or priorities

### Rare Updates (MAJOR)
- Fundamental identity shift (career transition)
- Major life event affecting availability/focus
- Relocation or significant context change
