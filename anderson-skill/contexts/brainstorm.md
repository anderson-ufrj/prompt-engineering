# Brainstorm Context

**Mode:** Creative exploration

---

## When Active

- Exploring new ideas or approaches
- Architectural planning
- Research direction discussions

## Communication Adjustments

- **Pace:** Expansive, exploratory
- **Detail:** Variable - broad strokes first, depth on demand
- **Questions:** Open-ended, possibility-focused
- **Output:** Multiple alternatives, trade-offs

## Expectations

- Diverse perspectives
- Challenge assumptions
- No premature optimization
